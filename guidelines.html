Important reminders

Instructions for Invigilators

Open exchange sheet
1

If leave is unavoidable, arrange another SWE faculty member to cover the duty and update the official exchange sheet before the exam date.

2

Collect answer scripts, question papers, attendance sheet, daily seat plan, top sheets, and other required materials from Room 619 at least 20 minutes before the exam begins.

3

Mobile phones are strictly prohibited in the exam hall for both students and faculty. Student violations may result in cancellation of the answer script.

4

Verify each student's ID card, admit card, clearance, and the course teacher's initials on the answer script before signing.

5

Carefully count and check all answer scripts immediately after receiving them. Match scripts with the attendance sheet and top sheet before submission.

6

Read the SWE Exam Policy for Faculty Members and the Exam Invigilation Training file before duty for smooth exam conduction.

7

For any query during the exam, call the exam committee directly. Do not post WhatsApp messages in the faculty group.
